Latitude and Humidity

There does not appear to be a particularly significant directional trend between latitude and humidity in the sampled cities -- except perhaps that most cities seem to have relatively higher (>60%) levels of humidity. It could be worth measuring humidity's relationship with other factors aside from latitude.
